Delete Your Account

Wannabe Cup (WBC) golf tournament app · Last updated July 10, 2026

You can delete your Wannabe Cup account and associated personal data at any time. This page explains how, and exactly what is removed and what is retained.

Option 1 — Delete in the app (fastest)

  1. Open the Wannabe Cup app and sign in.
  2. Tap your name in the top-right corner to open the account menu.
  3. Choose Delete account.
  4. Confirm. Your account is removed immediately.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you can't access the app, email privacy@wannabecup.com from the address associated with your account, with the subject "Account deletion request." We will verify your identity and complete the deletion within 30 days (usually much sooner).

What happens to your data

Deleted

  • Your Google / Apple login credential
  • Your stored email address
  • The link between your login and your player profile
  • Your push-notification device tokens

Retained

  • Historical tournament scores and statistics, kept as part of the Wannabe Cup's multi-year record book

Retained scores are de-identified: they stay attached to an internal player ID that is no longer connected to your email or login. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Deletion is permanent. If you rejoin a future tournament, you would set up a new account.
